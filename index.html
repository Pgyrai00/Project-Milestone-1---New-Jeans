About us

Lumen is a girl group blending minimal R&B, pop and electronic textures into one quiet, glowing sound.

Four voices, one outline. Lumen lives in the space between midnight and morning: restrained production, close harmonies, and lyrics that feel like short messages never sent. The sound is bright but measured, warm but clean, leaving room for every breath and echo.

On stage, the group keeps the design minimal – monochrome silhouettes, simple lines, and a quiet focus on the music itself. No clutter, no noise, just a slow build that makes the small details feel loud.

Shows are kept intentionally small and intimate: galleries, warehouses, rooftops – spaces where silence sounds as full as the chorus. Each set is arranged like a short film in three acts.

Lumen is designed to feel like a single photograph: one frame, one color palette, and everything placed on purpose.

Background

A few still frames from the spaces that shaped the band – rehearsal rooms, rooftops, and city light.

The group started as late-night demos traded between cities – minimal beats, half-finished verses, quiet phone recordings. Over time, the production sharpened but the core stayed small: soft chords, air between the notes, and vocals right up close.

Lumen keeps a visual world as simple as the sound: neutral tones, clean shapes, and a single accent color that comes and goes like light from a passing train.
